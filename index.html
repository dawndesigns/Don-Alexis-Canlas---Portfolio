About Me

I began my journey as a Valorant shoutcaster for a university organization, driven by my passion for esports and the thrill of highlighting unforgettable moments. With over two years of experience, I have worked on a wide range of titles including Valorant, League of Legends, Counter-Strike, DOTA 2, and NBA2K. Beyond esports, I have also provided commentary for traditional sports such as basketball, volleyball, MMA, and boxing.

Academically, I am a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate with a specialization in networking and cybersecurity. My academic background has equipped me with strong technical skills, while my professional experiences have allowed me to apply project execution and communication expertise in both technology and events management.

Recently, I gained hands-on experience as a Project Management Intern at Dark League Studios, supporting event coordination, asset tracking, and broadcast operations. I bring together technical proficiency, organizational skills, and storytelling to elevate the esports and events industry—both behind the scenes and in the spotlight.
